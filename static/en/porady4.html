Heart and Vessels: how to protect the most vulnerable body system against ageing and noci-influence?

Cardiovascular diseases are suffered worldwide, and scientists know about prevalence indexes dependency on these pathologies and dietary habits. It is well-known that many fat fish sorts are taken, heart and vessel diseases are spread the least.

Omega-3 polyunsaturated fatty acids (PUFA) are contained in the fish of fat sorts (these fish species inhabiting in cold seas). When scientists has decided to learn how acids of Omega-3 impact upon the vessels state they understood that PUFAs help reduce concretion on the vessel walls giving rise to atheromatous plaques. As a consequence, risk of such serious and life threatening states, like heart attacks and apoplectic attack, is reduced. In addition, it is proven long ago that Omega-3 polyunsaturated fatty acids effectively contribute to cholesterol level decrease – so-called "bad fats" being accumulated in the vessels and causing serious diseases.

It is also important that Omega-3 acids have anti-inflammatory effect – it is useful for vessel health maintenance and cardiac function support. Almost already 40 years doctors worldwide prescribe preparations containing Omega-3 for prevention and in complex treatment of cardiovascular diseases. In particular, Vitamin A, Vitamin C, Vitamin E as well as selenium, zinc, vitamins of B group are necessary for vessels health. All of them perform a definite action within the body. For example, Vitamins С and Е – are antioxidants providing health and youth of all body cells. Vitamin E also prevents appearance of thrombi, which is very important for cardiovascular system. Vitamin C reinforces vessel walls, regulates blood circulation.

Vitamins of B group positively influence upon the function of cardiac muscle (namely, vitamins В1, В2, В6) and of nervous system, which is extremely essential for cardiovascular diseases prevention.

Vitatone MultiOmega includes all these vitamins required to support hear functioning being in the complex of Omega-3 polyunsaturated fatty acids. This combination enables applying syrup by people of different age groups for heart diseases as well as supporting and regulation of cardiovascular system. Actually, Omega-3 "clean up" vessels from cholesterol, which in due course form indurations in the vessels causing acute critical states – heart or apoplectic attacks.

Considering that Omega-3 acids help reduce and keep arterial tension in norm, Vitatone MultiOmega syrup is also recommended for those having complaints on blood pressure increase or disposed to development of hypertensive disease.

Thus, Omega-3 – is a unique substance being in complex with vitamins and microelements favours avoiding atherogenesis, cholesterol plaques formation in the vessels, provides with an opportunity to maintain vessels compliance, ensures arterial tension regulation, prevents occurrence of heart and apoplectic attacks – the most dangerous cardiovascular diseases.
